# Hackathon + Dating Website
This is a hackathon + dating website that provides a unique platform for developers and technology enthusiasts to connect and find love. The website combines the fun and excitement of a hackathon with the thrill of online dating, providing a unique experience for users.

## Features
Users can create a profile and provide information about their skills, interests, and what they are looking for in a partner. 
Users can browse through the profiles of other members and connect with those who share similar interests and skills. 
Users can participate in hackathons organized by the website and work on projects with other members. 
Users can chat with each other through the website's messaging system. 
Users can receive notifications when new matches are found or when a new hackathon is organized.
